CMP6230 Coursework Task Sheet 4 Evaluation and Communication of Wider issues

Your fourth and final task will be to answer a set of **questions** based on those provided below to

demonstrate your research and developing knowledge into some of the wider concerns of implementing Machine Learning and Data Science techniques in industry. This will be documented within your log

report.

The purpose of this task is to contribute towards meeting the module learning outcome: “Evaluate and communicate the ethical and legal issues which underpin concerns related to the security and privacy of data”

Underlined are some of the key parts of the learning outcome, but what do they mean?

* Evaluate and communicate

  * Demonstrate the ability to judge a given concept, technique or event backed by appropriate usage of evidence

* Ethical

  * What moral implications are there behind how data may be used?

* Legal

  * What legislation exists to attempt to limit the inappropriate handling of data?

* Security

  * How can data and the systems that process data be protected from unauthorised access and usage?

* Privacy

  * How does the manner that data is handled effectively allow people to control how personal information is handled?

# **Exercise 4: Step 2**

Select **one** of the below questions and write a short essay attempting to explore and answer the question presented (backed by appropriate references):

1. What techniques might a production OLTP database administration team commonly use to help secure their relational database management system in an enterprise setting?  
2. What benefits does the subfield of Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning hope to provide?

3. What are the origins of the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR), what did regulators hope to achieve when implementing it, and what has been its impact on organisations?

4. What is Differential Privacy and what examples of real-world applications of this concept have been implemented thus far?

5. What is k-anonymity and how can it help to prevent the re-identification of individuals within a given set of data?

6. What is Synthetic Data and what are the common methods that are currently leveraged to attempt to perform Synthetic Data Generation?

7. What was the Facebook-Cambridge Analytica Data Scandal and how was the use of Data considered to be an inappropriate breach of users' trust?

8. In the Data Governance and Data Management policy of an organisation, what is the common role and purpose of a Data Steward? How does Data Stewardship commonly contrast/complement

   Data Curation?
